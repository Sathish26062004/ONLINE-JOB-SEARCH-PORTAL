Industrial Companies

General Electric

General Electric (GE) is an American multinational conglomerate incorporated in New York City and headquartered in Boston. It operates through various segments including aviation, healthcare, power, renewable energy, digital industry, and more.

Founded: April 15, 1892

Headquarters: Boston, Massachusetts, United States

CEO: Larry Culp

Siemens

Siemens AG is a German multinational conglomerate company headquartered in Munich and Berlin. It operates in various sectors including industry, energy, healthcare, and infrastructure & cities.

Founded: October 12, 1847

Headquarters: Munich, Germany

CEO: Roland Busch

Lockheed Martin

Lockheed Martin Corporation is an American aerospace, defense, arms, security, and advanced technologies company headquartered in North Bethesda, Maryland. It is one of the largest defense contractors in the world.

Founded: March 15, 1995

Headquarters: North Bethesda, Maryland, United States

CEO: James D. Taiclet
