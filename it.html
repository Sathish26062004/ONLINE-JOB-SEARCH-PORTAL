IT Companies

Microsoft

Microsoft Corporation is an American multinational technology company that develops, manufactures, licenses, supports, and sells computer software, consumer electronics, personal computers, and related services.

Founded: April 4, 1975

Headquarters: Redmond, Washington, United States

CEO: Satya Nadella

Google

Google LLC is an American multinational technology company that specializes in Internet-related services and products, which include online advertising technologies, a search engine, cloud computing, software, and hardware.

Founded: September 4, 1998

Headquarters: Mountain View, California, United States

CEO: Sundar Pichai

Amazon

Amazon.com, Inc. is an American multinational technology company based in Seattle, Washington, which focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence.

Founded: July 5, 1994

Headquarters: Seattle, Washington, United States

CEO: Jeff Bezos
